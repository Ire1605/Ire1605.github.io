About me

Research Focus

I'm a PhD researcher specialising in robotics and computer vision, with a focus on giving autonomous systems the ability to actively explore, understand, and reason about their environment.

My current research centres on object recognition in warehouse environments — building robust perception pipelines that work across varied conditions, object categories, and spatial layouts.

I'm particularly passionate about active vision: the question of where a robot should look next to maximise its understanding of a scene, spanning next-best-view planning, 3D reconstruction, and semantic mapping.
